SCHOOLS AND DEPARTMENT

Penn State Technical College have four schools.


The school of pure and applied sciences(SPAS ) aims at producing professional scientists for careers in industry, teaching and research. The school focuses on imparting knowledge in areas of chemical biological,actuarial and information technology sciences.
The departments in this school include:
(i)department of computer science
(ii)department of physical science
(iii)department of biological sciences.

The school of Business(SoB)aims at producing managers and business leaders of international repute. We strive to achieve this through imparting our students with the best management and enterpreneurial skills that are globally accepted.
This school has four departments;
(i)department of Business and enterprenuership
(ii)department of Human Resource
(iii)department of Economics
(iv)department of Tourism and Hospitality Management

The school of education and social sciences aims at producing graduates who will serve in various capacities in the education sector, social welfare organizations and government agencies locally and internatinally.
This school has five departments:
(i)department of Humanities and Languages
(ii)department of Social Sciences
(iii)department of Education Foundation
(iv)department of Planning and Curriculum
(v) department of Psychology and Comunication technology

The School of Beauty and Fashion

is a school that focuses on the students skills and trending fashion.Through beauty our students are able to work even after school as a practice for improvement. It is very important to give out Beauty and Fashion Proffesionals.This school is quite a big field in which any student can major his/her area of interest.Our programmes ensure graduates are equipped with relevant intellectual skills to the needs of society
